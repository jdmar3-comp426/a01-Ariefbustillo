THE LORD OF THE RINGS

The Lord of the Rings was a set of three novels written by J.R.R. Tolkein. It was later adapted into a three part film trilogy directed by Peter Jackson. It starred Viggo Mortenson, Elijah Wood, and Ian McKellan, among others. The Hobbit was a single novel written by Tolkein, and serves as a prequel to the Lord of the Rings. It was then made into three movies (more movies than necessary). They weren't great.
